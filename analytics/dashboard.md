The main entry point of the dashboard. Provides a high-level summary of sales performance across time, product, and geography.

### KPI Cards

Four KPI cards at the top of the page display the core business metrics, each with a Month-over-Month (MoM) % indicator:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Sales | Total revenue generated |
| Cost | Total cost of goods sold |
| Profit | Total profit (Sales - Cost) |
| Margin | Overall profit margin % |

### Revenue vs Profit

Area chart displaying Total Sales and Total Profit over 12 months. Tracks the relationship between revenue and profitability over time, highlighting months where margins expanded or compressed.

### Total Sales by Product Category

Donut chart breaking down sales into Bikes, Accessories, and Clothing. 

### Total Sales by Product Line

Horizontal bar chart showing performance across product lines (Mountain, Road, Touring, Other). Provides a more granular product breakdown compared to the category view.

### Total Sales by Country

Horizontal bar chart displaying sales distribution across key markets. Identifies top-performing geographies and enables market size comparison.

### Filters

Custom filter panel accessed via **Filters** button (bookmark-based) with Year, Month, Quarter, and Full Date slicers.
